# RFP-2026-CLOUD: Cloud migration services
Acme Corporation is requesting proposals for Cloud Migration Services.

## 1. Eligibility & Mandatory Requirements
The contractor MUST possess ISO 27001 Information Security Management certification.
The contractor must have a minimum of 5 years of experience in enterprise Kubernetes deployments.
All proposed engineers must hold active AWS Solution Architect Professional certificates.

## 2. Evaluation Criteria
Proposal suitability will be evaluated as follows:
- Technical Approach and Experience: 40% of the total score.
- Pricing structure and rates: 30% of the total score.
- Case studies and references: 30% of the total score.

## 3. Mandatory Documents for Submission
Bidders must submit:
- Copy of ISO 27001 Certificate
- Resumes of proposed Key Personnel
- Case studies of 3 similar projects completed in the past 3 years

## 4. Key Deadlines
- Proposal Submission Deadline: July 15, 2026, at 5:00 PM EST.
- Q&A Submission Deadline: June 30, 2026.
